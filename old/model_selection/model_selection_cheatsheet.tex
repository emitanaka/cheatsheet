\section{Akaike Information Criteria}
$$2 \times \text{log-likelihood} + 2p $$
BIC is consistent in selecting the true model.
AIC is minimax-rate optimal for estimating the regression function.

You cannot have both consistency and minimax-rate optimality (Yang 2005)
